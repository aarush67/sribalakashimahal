About Sri Balakashi Mahal

Nestled in Thekkalur, Coimbatore, Sri Balakashi Mahal is a modern, air-conditioned venue crafted for unforgettable weddings and events. Our serene temple on-site adds a touch of tradition to your celebrations.

With citywide bus access and included amenities like vessels, tables, chairs, and luxurious A/C spaces, we ensure every detail is perfect.
