About Sri BalaKashi Mahal

Sri BalaKashi Mahal in Thekkalur, Coimbatore, offers newly built, air-conditioned halls and modern facilities, perfect for weddings and events.

Bus facilities available from all parts of the city. Rent includes vessels, tables, chairs, two A/C halls, and two air-conditioned rooms.
