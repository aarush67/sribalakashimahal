About Sri Balakashi Mahal

Nestled in Thekkalur, Coimbatore, Sri Balakashi Mahal is a modern, air-conditioned venue crafted for unforgettable weddings and events. Our serene on-site temple adds a touch of tradition to your celebrations.

With citywide bus access and included amenities like vessels, tables, chairs, and luxurious A/C spaces, we ensure every detail is perfect for your special day.

Our Amenities

Ambience

  • Spacious air-conditioned Marriage Hall for up to 1,000 guests
  • Elegant air-conditioned Dining Hall with seating for 350 guests
  • Two luxurious air-conditioned rooms for the bride and groom
  • Ample parking space surrounding the venue
  • Standby generator and inverter for uninterrupted comfort
  • Serene temple within the premises for traditional ceremonies

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art kitchen with gas stoves
  • Steam cookers for rice, dal, vegetables, sambar, and milk
  • Industrial-grade Eureka Forbes water purifier
  • Complimentary vessels, tables, and chairs included
  • Convenient bus services from all parts of the city
  • Expert assistance for decorations, catering, and more

Rental package includes vessels, tables, chairs, two A/C halls, and two air-conditioned rooms.
