Located in the heart of Thekkalur, Coimbatore, Sri Balakashi Mahal has been the trusted setting for generations of families marking their most important milestones.

Our venue blends the warmth of Tamil tradition with modern comfort. The sprawling marriage hall accommodates up to 1,000 guests in fully air-conditioned splendor, while the dining hall seats 350 for a smooth, unhurried feast. A serene on-site temple means your rituals and reception unfold in one blessed place.

We handle the details so you can be fully present. Vessels, tables, chairs, both halls, and two private bridal suites are all included, letting you focus entirely on the joy of the occasion.

Facilities Designed for Flawless Events

Every amenity has been thoughtfully chosen to make your event run smoothly, from the first guest's arrival to the last farewell.

Ambience and Comfort

  • Spacious air-conditioned marriage hall for up to 1,000 guests
  • Elegant air-conditioned dining hall with seating for 350 guests
  • Two private air-conditioned suites for the bride and groom
  • Ample parking space surrounding the venue for all guests
  • Standby generator and inverter for uninterrupted comfort
  • Serene temple within the premises for traditional ceremonies

Kitchen and Catering

  • State-of-the-art kitchen fully equipped with commercial gas stoves
  • Industrial steam cookers for rice, dal, vegetables, sambar, and milk
  • Industrial-grade Eureka Forbes water purification system
  • Complimentary vessels, serving utensils, tables, and chairs included
  • Expert assistance available for decorations and catering arrangements
  • Convenient bus services connecting all parts of the city to our venue
